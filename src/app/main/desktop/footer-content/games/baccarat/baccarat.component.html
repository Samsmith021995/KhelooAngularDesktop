✪ Baccarat Game Rules

Baccarat is a comparing card game, which is played among a banker and a player. Every baccarat round of play will have 3 probable results:

  • Banker

  • Player (she/he has a greater score)

The game has 3 famous variants:

  • Baccarat banque also called a deux tableaux
