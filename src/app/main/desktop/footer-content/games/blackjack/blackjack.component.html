✪ Blackjack Game Rules

The main aim of blackjack is to get to the numerical 21 or as close to it as they can in value of the cards. The number cards are all evaluated at their face value. While all the character cards are worth 10. Keep in mind that Ace could either mean 1 or 11.

Regardless of what variation of blackjack that you might play, it’s crucial to keep a few of the rules in mind.

  • Always divide your 8’s & Aces - aces hold a value of 11, so the players have a greater possibility of getting a strong hand if they divide them. Two 8’s have a collective value of 16, which is recognised as a weak total in blackjack. Drawing any card valued high or at 6 will result in a bust, so dividing them is the only way to go.

  • Consider carefully before doubling down - the move might seem enticing, but it’ll restrict your flexibility. Evade it if your hand sums around 18 or 19, if the dealer’s card is 11, 10 or 9 or if you are on a limited budget.

  • Insuring guarantees no losses - this side bet can safeguard you from losing your beginning wage if you forecast the dealer’s down card correctly. Even so, it's a highly risky choice in the long term, as the odds that the dealer’s down card has a value of 10 are normally less than 2:1. Additionally, you’ll confront a greater house edge.
