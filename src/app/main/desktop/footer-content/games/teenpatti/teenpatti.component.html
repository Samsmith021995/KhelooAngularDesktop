Mufliss - Lowball or Mufliss is a Teen Patti variant that utilises a reverse hand ranking to the classic game. Here, the greatest hand is the High card, while the weakest is the Set or Trial.

Sudden Death - in this variation of Teen Patti, the dealer allocates the complete 52 deck of cards among the players. After the deals have been made, the player drops cards from their hands on the table periodically till one of the gamers declare Stop!. Every player then picks three of the greatest ranking cards from their hand and differentiates them to those of the rest. The greatest hand succeeds.

High Wild - the variant of 3 Patti showcases all traditional rules excluding one. It permits players to switch their lower ranked cards for any other card they prefer and come up with a better hand.

Little King - here, your lowest ranking card and all king cards are jokers. They can have any value you desire to aid you fulfil the hand that you already have.

Cobra - this unconventional version is played with one hole card per gamer. Every player places the card they have received on their foreheads so that they are unable to see it, but the other players can. The winner is the player with the greatest ranked card.
