How To Withdraw?

Step 1:

Login to your kheloo account and click to profile button and click on 'WITHDRAWS'.

Step 2:

Enter your bank account details and amount you want to withdraw and click to 'SUBMIT'.

Step 3:

Then It's show success message then click to 'OKay'

Step 4:

Now select 'Withdrawal Status' tab and check your Withdrawal status.
